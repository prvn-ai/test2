About Madurai

Madurai is among the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities and a major cultural center of Tamil civilization situated on the banks of the Vaigai River.

Temples & Heritage

Meenakshi Amman Temple, Koodal Azhagar Temple and Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal represent the city's architectural and spiritual legacy.

Culture & Traditions

Madurai is the heartland of Tamil language, Sangam literature, Bharatanatyam, Carnatic music and the famous Chithirai Festival.

Food & Flavours

Known for Jigarthanda, Kari Dosa, Parotta and authentic Tamil cuisine, Madurai is one of South India's most celebrated food destinations.
